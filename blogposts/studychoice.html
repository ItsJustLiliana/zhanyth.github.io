Study Choice

From a young age I already knew what I would like to become in the future. It was clear to
me what my path was and how I would be walking it. It started with turning on and off
computers at primary school and even on (request) login into my teachers computer before they
arrived in the classroom. After that on high school I started doing some messing with the
schoolcomputers which was a lot of fun.

Now I am incredibly interested in the whole science behind it and how computers operate. I've been
able to dissasemble some pc's and take a look inside them. All the little components I wanted
to keep were still a motivation to this day to find out what each of them does.

I got a Nintendo 3DS since I was 8 years old and these days it doesn't have any support anymore
which means there are no update to the software, new games being published and all official
online services have been taken down which is all the more reason to look into modding your
3DS. One day I stumbled upon a video of someone explaining how to mod your 3DS and I was
immediately interested. It took a bit of time to make it happen on my own console, but
eventually I made it happen. Now I want to know how all of this is even possible, because
if you make one wrong move your console might get bricked and unable to be restored. It is
so interesting how all of it works.
