Frequently Asked Questions

Provides answers to your general questions!

You have to connect your laptop to "HZ printer" by going to hz.mynetpay.nl. Then You will have to log in with your HZ account, and then you can use the printer.

Open Windows Fax or MacOS and place your document faced down on the scanner bed of the HZ printer. After that select scan and the document should appear on your device. From there you can save it.

You first have to log into the HZ webshop with your HZ account. After that you can add an item to your shopping cart by clicking "Bestel". Then you can go to your shopping cart and click on "Afrekenen". After the payment has been completed, your item will be ordered.

You have to go to portal.hz.nl and then slecect "Selfservice Portal". Then click on "Maak een reservering". Here you can select a room you want to book.

To enter the parking lot, you must have your HZ card with you to be admitted. After that you follow the signs pointing towards HZ parking spaces. At these parking spaces you can park your car.
